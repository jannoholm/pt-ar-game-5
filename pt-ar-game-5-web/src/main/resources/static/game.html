HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to TRIVIASPREE, a fast paced quiz game to test your knowledge in different fields.

You will be challenged by numerous mind bending questions and must choose from one of four different answer. For these choices we provide you with a modest 120 seconds, so things don't get too boring. As with everything in life so can also the questions have more than one correct answer. Every correct answer rewards you with 100 points. Consecutive correct answers build up a combo multiplier for a chance to hexaply your points but be cautious, wrong answers set you back 2 levels.

Good luck and have fun ripping and tearing through the questions.

BE WARNED Mindless mashing will get you nowhere but off the leaderboard. This is a courteous game where speed of mind, not mashing of your index finger is put to the test.
